About Turtles

Turtles are reptiles that have been around for over 200 million years. They have a hard protective shell on their back, which they can retract their head, legs, and tail into for protection. Turtles are found in various aquatic and terrestrial habitats around the world.

Turtle Species

  • Green sea turtle
  • Loggerhead turtle
  • Box turtle
  • Painted turtle

Turtle Care

Turtles require a suitable environment to thrive. This includes a tank or pond with proper filtration and heating. They also need a balanced diet of protein and vegetables. Turtles can live for many decades if properly cared for.
